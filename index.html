I am an AI Research Scientist at Meta Superintelligence Lab, focusing on the cutting edge of interactive multimodal intelligence. Previously, I led human-centric generation efforts at ByteDance, following my work in perception at Alibaba.

My work in multimedia perception, spanning recognition, temporal localization, and spatio-temporal detection, achieved six winner awards in CVPR challenges. In generation, my contributions, including Loopy, the OmniHuman series, Seaweed, and Seedance, have been deployed in products serving billions of users worldwide, and these works have been covered by Forbes, ABC, a16z, Wired, Business Insider, VentureBeat, CNR News, Synced, and others.

I enjoy working with small, agile, dense teams that operate like startups, exploring new framings of a problem from both product and research views. I am currently exploring how to build new forms and experiences of interactive intelligence.
